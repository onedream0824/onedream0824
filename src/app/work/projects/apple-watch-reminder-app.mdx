## Overview

Built an Apple Watch-focused reminder app to help users stay on top of tasks, meetings, and important events throughout the day.

## Key Features

- **Timely reminders** with haptic taps and watch notifications
- **Quick glance interactions** designed for wearable-first usage
- **Daily planning support** for tasks and scheduled events

## Technologies Used

- **Apple Watch**
- **SwiftUI**
- **iOS Development**

## Outcome

Delivered a lightweight, wearable-friendly productivity companion that improves consistency and reduces missed events.

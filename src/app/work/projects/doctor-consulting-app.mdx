## Overview

Built a telemedicine app for Android and iOS to support secure remote consultations between patients and healthcare providers.

## Key Features

- **Appointment booking workflows**
- **Video consultation support**
- **Medical records access**
- **Prescription delivery support**
- **Secure communication between patients and doctors**

## Technologies Used

- **Flutter**
- **React Native**
- **Node.js**
- **Python**
- **Android and iOS development**

## Outcome

Delivered a user-friendly digital consultation platform that improves healthcare accessibility with mobile-first remote care workflows.
